Our

History

2007 – Foundation

Jorge Alor establishes BNN, a digital agency focused on innovation and technology, positioning itself as a leader in digital marketing in Mexico.

2013 – Recognition

BNN receives the "Socially Devoted" recognition from Social Bakers, standing out for its excellent digital customer service with the Nestlé campaign.

2019 - Partnership with OCESA

BNN partners with OCESA, expanding its ability to deliver digital experiences and entertainment by integrating cutting-edge technology.

2020 – Expansion

BNN solidifies its presence in new industries such as food and entertainment, launching proprietary products like Location Media, which combines physical and digital experiences.

2024 – Continuous Innovation

Today, at BNN, we continue to shape the marketing of the future, adapting to the present and leading the digital transformation.
